About Us

This web app was created to help people achieve their goals. It is common knowledge that very New Years Day many people across the world set goals they want to achieve by the end of the year, but rarely do peolpe achieve these goals. There are various reasons why these goals are nver achieved, but the most common reason is that people do not have a plan to achieve these goals. This web app was created to help you set your goals and remind you to achieve them.
